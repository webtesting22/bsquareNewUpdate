5 Reasons Why Flexible Office Spaces and Co-Working Spaces are The Next Big Thing



April 12, 2019

Flexible office spaces and co-working spaces are a rage in all the big and small cities. There are a lot of reasons for the same. Managing a huge office space becomes tiring as you have to look after a lot of things. This distracts you from your goal and has an adverse effect on your productivity as well.

Here are 5 reasons why flexible office spaces and co-working spaces are much better than opting for your usual offices.

1. Fully serviced and manageable offices spaces When you rent a normal office space, you have to take care of everything. From electricity, staff, pantry, furniture, Wi-Fi, water and many more miscellaneous expenditures and this makes it difficult for you to concentrate on your own work. The advantage of being a part of co-working and flexible office spaces that you don’t have to worry about these things. There is a whole team involved in managing these things.

The office space is also fully-furnished and well-equipped with all the necessary amenities.

2. Provision of staff members for office work, pantry, hygiene, reception etc. At a normal office, you are responsible for hiring and handling the staff members required for different jobs like office work, pantry, cleaning, technicians and reception but at a co-working spaces or flexible office spaces, these things are handled by the team of that particular coworking space. This way, you don’t have to bother about anything. You just have to focus on your work.

3. Flexible rent/lease options At a co-working space, the biggest benefit of it all is that you don’t have to bother with fixed contracts or lease options. Your company can grow as per your need and requirements. You could start with a desk and have a huge office space when you go through a change. You can book an office space for a day, week or months and this cost-effective flexibility is what helps you grow without worrying about the monetary aspect.

4. Meeting like-minded people and collaborating with the best The biggest advantage of being in a co-working space is that you get to meet a lot of like-minded people from different backgrounds and fields of work. You also get to collaborate with them and be part of events that are innovative and impressive. Who knows, you might meet your future team through a co-working space and that might make a huge difference in your career.

5. Flexible booking of meeting rooms for conferences, training and workshops etc. Now conducting meetings, conferences, workshops, training sessions and other interactive events has become super easy because of such office spaces. You can rent these spaces very easily and they are well-equipped with all facilities and amenities required for the completion of such events.

Conclusion:
In the fast-paced age of technology and advancements, it is extremely important for us to be updated and resonant with the on-going trends and practices. People don’t like to be bound by unnecessary hassles. One of the reasons of co-working spaces being a huge hit is the fact that these are cost-effective and easy to deal with. This makes a huge difference when it comes to attracting the right kind of audience.

If you are looking for such a place in Ahmedabad then you should surely check out BSQUARE Coworking Space. It has a lot of options like Private Offices, Co-working Spaces, Meeting Spaces for start-ups, corporate companies, MNCs, freelancers and many more.

The Co-working Revolution: Transforming Real Estate and Redefining Workspaces



October 26, 2018

The world of real estate is undergoing a significant transformation, and at the heart of this change is the booming trend of co-working spaces. Co-working is no longer just a buzzword; it's a powerful force reshaping the way we think about office spaces, property investments, and the future of work. During the first quarter of 2023, the co-working segment’s share saw a 27 percent increase to 2.18 million square feet across the top seven cities, according to ANAROCK. In this blog post, we'll delve into the co-working trend in real estate and explore how it's revolutionizing the industry.

The Rise of Co-working in Real Estate
Co-working spaces have become a prominent trend in the real estate sector, and for good reason. This innovative approach to workspace design offers a range of benefits for both property owners and tenants, making it a win-win scenario for all involved. Among the top seven cities, Bengaluru and NCR together accounted for 66 percent (around 1.43 million sq. ft) of net coworking absorption in Q1 2023. While Pune and Chennai together stood at around 0.52 million sq. ft of coworking space in the same period. Co-working spaces offer numerous benefits, including cost-efficiency by sharing overhead expenses, flexibility with short-term leases, and access to premium amenities like high-speed internet and meeting rooms. They foster a collaborative environment, encouraging networking and the exchange of ideas among diverse professionals. Prime locations enhance accessibility and business image, while the community aspect reduces isolation for remote workers. Overall, co-working spaces provide a dynamic and adaptable workspace solution that suits the needs of modern professionals and businesses.

Co-working as a Future-Proof Real Estate Investment
The co-working trend in real estate isn't merely a short-lived fad; it's a fundamental shift in how we approach work and property investments. As remote work and flexible schedules become increasingly prevalent, the demand for co-working spaces is expected to grow. This makes co-working properties a future-proof investment that can adapt to the changing market

Conclusion: Coworking's Enduring Impact
Co-working spaces are no longer just a trend; they are a dynamic and transformative force in the world of real estate with the prominent players in the market like Bsquare, WeWork, DevX Cowork, IndiQube, 91SpringBoard, ABL Workspaces, Workafella, The Address, Smart Work, Regus, Awfis, Opulence, Karma Workspace, and Innov8. Property owners, co-working operators, and tenants all stand to benefit from the co-working revolution. As the lines between work and lifestyle continue to blur, co-working spaces are becoming the preferred choice for a diverse range of professionals and businesses. Embracing this trend can lead to prosperous real estate investments and thriving workspaces that cater to the evolving needs of the workforce.
